Air Travel

The island's cozy airport accommodates small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is expanding its airport to handle larger jets, with the project set to finish within the next few years.

Ground Travel

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily. Taxis and rental cars are available, along with bike rentals. Taniti City is flat and walkable, with many tourists exploring Merriton Landing on foot. Private buses serve areas outside the city as well. For those who want to drive, rental cars can be found from several vendors.

Location: 23432 Neodog Ct., Klendathu, Taniti
